The Avengers - Details


Robert Downey Jr. plays Tony Stark/Iron Man. His character is, "a billionaire playboy philanthropist."
Chris Hemsworth plays Thor, the Asgardian warrior who wields Mjolnir, his hammer. his character is the half-brother of Loki, the main antagonist in the movie.
Chris Evans plays Captain America, a super-soldier from the 1940's who was frozen in the Arctic before SHIELD revived him. Mark Ruffalo plays Bruce Banner/Hulk, a gamma radiation scientist who after a freak accident became the Hulk, a insanely strong creature. His character must constantly keep his anger in check lest he transform into the Hulk
Scarlett Johansson plays Black Widow, a russian-born SHIELD agent with a dark past. her character occasionally works alongside Hawkeye, another SHIELD agent. Her character has no powers other than her own skills, which are powerful in their own right.
Jeremy Renner plays Hawkeye, a SHIELD agent who is extremely skilled archer, though not an ordinary one. His character doesn't have powers, but he is a deadeye shot with a bow, a power in its own right.

The Avengers was released April 11, 2012, and grossed $623,357,910 in the USA as of April 27, 2013
