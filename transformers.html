Transformers - Details


Shia LeBeouf plays Sam Whitwicky, a teenager who happens to be caught up in a war between the Autobots and the Decepticons. Megan Fox plays Mikaela Banes, a girl who gets caught up in the same war Sam is. Josh Duramel plays Captain William Lennox, a US Army Ranger. Tyrese Gibson plays USAF Tech Sergant Epps, a friend of Lennox. Jon Voight plays Defense Secretary John Keller, he is in charge of classified projects involving transformers. John Turturro plays Agent Simmons, an agent with Sector 7. Michael O'Neill plays Tom Banacheck, he is the head of Sector 7.

Transformers was released July 3, 2007 and grossed $318,759,914 in the USA as of October 26, 2007.
