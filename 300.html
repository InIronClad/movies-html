300 - Details


Gerard Butler plays King Leonidas, king of Sparta. He leads a group of 300 men to hold back the Persian army at Thermopolyae. Queen Gorgo is the queen of Sparta, wed to Leonidas. Xerxes is the god-king of Persia, he attacked the Greeks in hopes of accuiring their land.

This movie was released in December 9, 2006 and grossed $210,592,590 in the USA as of July6, 2007
