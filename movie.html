The Avengers


The Avengers is about a group of people with extraordinary abilities or gifts. This group is called "The Avengers" and they are part of SHIELD, an organization charged with the protection of Earth. When Loki, a being from Asgard, utilizes something called the Tessaract to travel to Earth. His plan is to take over it. SHIELD tries to stop him, but he is too powerful. The Avengers, Iron Man, Capt. America, The Hulk, Thor, Black Widow, and Hawkeye attempt to stop Loki, and in the end they do succeed, partly because Hulk smashes him into the ground repeatedly. After they win they part ways, but SHIELD knows if something happens again, they'll be back.

Guardians of the Galaxy


Guardians of the Galaxy is about a rogue who, while on a seemingly routine job, ends up with an artifact with unimaginable power. Because of this, he is dragged into a war, along with four other rogues, including a raccoon-like being named Rocket and a living tree named Groot. The five of them fight to stop an evil warlord from destroying the galaxy. In the end, they succeed.

300


300 is a dramatic telling of the story of 300 Spartans who fought the Persian army at Thermopylae. 300 Spartans attempted to hold back the massive advancing Persian army. They deal a tremendous blow to the Persians, but in the end they all are killed. The Spartans are, however, successful in delaying the Persians and giving the rest of Greece time to build defenses and countermeasures prepare to fend off the Persians.

Transformers


Transformers is about a young man who happens to encounter aliens composed of technology. These aliens have the ability to "transform" into a vehicle, allowing them to hide in plain sight blend in. The boy, Sam is caught up on a mission to defeat Megatron, an evil Transformer, and protect an artifact, called the Spark. In the end, he is instrumental in taking Megatron down.
