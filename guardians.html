Guardians of the Galaxy - Details


Chris Pratt plays Peter Quill, also known as Star-Lord, a rogue who later becomes the leader of a rag-tag group. Zoe Saldana plays Gamora, an alien assassin with cybernetic implants, she teams up with Star-Lord and others while in a prison. Dave Batista plays Drax the Destroyer, an alien whose family was killed by Thanos, and swears revenge against him. While in a prison, he meets Star-Lord and Gamora. Vin Diesel voices Groot, a sentient tree-like alien who is the muscle between him and Rocket, a cybernetically enhanced, genetically altered raccoon. Bradley Cooper voices Rocket, a raccoon who is genetically and cybernetically altered. He is the weapons guy. Lee Pace plays Ronan the Accuser, a Kree who vows to destroy Xandar, and who is being helped by Thanos. Josh Brolin plays Thanos, a superhuman being working in the shadows. His goal is unknown.

This movie was released August 1, 2014 and grossed $319,169,216 in the USA as of September 26, 2014.
